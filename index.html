Steps to follow

  • Step 1

    Contact your captain first

    Discuss your trip and agree on a price

  • Step 2

    Go to the captains reservation channels

    Your captain should have refered you to a specififc one (eg.0x363d)

  • Step3

    verify that trip information corresponds to you

    And input the amount to be deposited, then press reserve. You should input the exact amount noted!

  • Step 4

    After reserving, wait for captain to confirm your reservation

    If captain has not confirmed yet, you can press the cancel button to retrieve back your funds.

  • Step 5

    Once captain has confirmed your reservation

    You should press the approve trip button once you have physically met your captain.

  • Step 6

    Pressing the approve button will send the captain his fare,

    And you will receive back your part of the deposit you made. (fare = deposit - price)

  • Step 7

    If for any reason you dont want to approve the trip

    You can request a refund, which the captain has to confirm in order for both to get back their deposited funds.
