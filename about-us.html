About Us

Who are we?

We are tech enthusiasts, crypto-nerds, and, especially important, students currently studying at Assumption University, located in Thailand. As it is our senior year, it was required of us to create a senior project in order to complete our education. The long burdensome task of creating any application was hard enough but we sought to tinker into the realm of Web 3.0. It was a difficult enduring task yet it was fulfilling right to the end. The knowledge gained and fun while we were at it was an experience never to forget. Anyways we hope that you will enjoy the application whether it be for any purposes.

Why we chose this project?

As we are Information Technology students, building our own application is in a broad spectrum as the possibility is quite limitless. The types of projects that can be completed are endless and when choosing one, it should be completed in a careful manner. We thought hard and plenty of the choices that were available but none really intrigued us too much. Until we were approached with the idea to create a Web 3.0 application. Again the boundaries are limitless but at least it became about maybe the next big thing in our generation, blockchain and crypto.
